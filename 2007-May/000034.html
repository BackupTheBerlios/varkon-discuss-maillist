I have set up The Lofting Floor as a place where the Varkon CAD 
developers can get together to work on various parts of the Varkon 
system. The name reflects the nautical history of some of the techniques 
used in CAD and modeling.

Go there via the "Forum" link at Varkon.Org.

I thought this might be more useful than a plain mailing-list because 
you can attach images and code to your posts. You can also add 
categories and threads to the forum.

As an example, I've posted Dr. Kjellander's initial icon designs to the 
Icons board, assuming others will want to view what's there and post 
their own ideas.

Naturally, you will decide whether it replaces the mailing-list, or is 
an adjunct, or whether it's even useful.

I'm new at this, so bear with me, and feel free to make suggestions how 
to improve things.

Only registered users can post to the forum.
