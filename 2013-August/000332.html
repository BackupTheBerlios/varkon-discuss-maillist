Pierre,

This error is most probably related to text fonts in X-windows.

During startup, Varkon reads one or more ini-files. In your case
/home/pierre/Varkon_1.19E/cnf/ini/english/linux
according to the log in your mail.

The first lines in the ini-file specifies which X-windows fonts Varkon
should use. Maybe your machine does not have these installed ? If you
remove (comment) these lines Varkon will use some default which will not
look nice but at least let you get the system up and running.
