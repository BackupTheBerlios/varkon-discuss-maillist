Hi Ruven and others,

The job Ruven is doing in promoting Varkon is
absolutely wonderful. We do maintain the system
at Örebro university but we have no resources at
all to do anything more than that.

Varkon development has taken up speed during the
last 6 months. This is partly because of one more
PhD student (Mohamed Rahayem) but also because I
myself have more time for Varkon now than I had
while I was doing my time as the Head of the
Department.

Due to this, we have decided to write a new User
Interface for Varkon. This is a really big job,
and I can't say today when it will be finished.
I hope to release 1.19B before summer but there will
be more to do before the final version which I
suppose we will call 2.0. During this time focus
will be on the GUI and other things will have
lower priority.

The idea of a bug tracking system or even a source
repository at sourceforge is good. I think Armin
Faltl proposed this a year ago. The only reason
we don't have it is that nobody here has the time
to learn how to set it up. If somebody did that I
would gladly help as much as I could.

Regards, Johan
