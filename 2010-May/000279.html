John,

Unfortunately there is no way to call IGcptw() from a macro. (It would
be easy to add though.) What people do is to write their own dialogs
using MBS/Macro. IGcptw() works for all parameters in all parts. For
specific parameters in specific parts it may be more efficient to have
specific dialogs.

> > called more than
> >
