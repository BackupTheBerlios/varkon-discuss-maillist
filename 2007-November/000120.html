Hi folks
   We are blocked from porting varkon to the new Debian ARM EABI port
wiki.debian.org/ArmEabiPort by the fact that it needs
-fwritable-strings.
   That option died in gcc-3.3 (and I knew it was a mistake at the
time) and there is absolutely no hope of us ever getting gcc-3.3 or
indeed gcc-3.4 on ARM EABI; the earliest we can use is gcc-4.1.
   Is there any chance of getting the need for writeable-strings
removed from varkon at the source? It is the only remaining software
package in Debian, out of 7000, that still needs gcc-3.3.

Cheers

    M
