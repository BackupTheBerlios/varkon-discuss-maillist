to add, that when Varkon is run by a script (not running a script)

About my own work on the demo I implemented the buttons and
some callbacks for spline manipulation this weekend. The interior
is still missing but read in of interpolation or control points for
creation would work. I yet have to come up with a spec on how
draging of control points should work. As of now I have a 'DRAG'
button, that shall trigger auto-selection of the spline and nearest
control point on it. Maybe I implement a simple polyline, to give
you an impression of how it might work.
