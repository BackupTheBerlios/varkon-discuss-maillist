To give more detail on that:

surface/surface intersections have been implemented by me in last 
fall/winter
for the general case of parametric surfaces (including NURBS), but lacking
the handling of bounds of the intersection curve, caused by boundaries of
the surfaces. The later exists as detailed algorithm description.
The implementation was tested on a cylinder/cylinder mockup and takes 
only ms.
The implementation/combination of this feature with Varkon surfaces
depends on the possiblity to express the intersection curve as a parametric
curve on a surface in the u,v-space of that surface, which will be possible
with the trimmed-surface feature.
