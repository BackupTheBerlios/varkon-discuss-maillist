Hello Glenn



The (generalized) conic implemented in Varkon was originally developed by 
Professor Alan Ball (Birmingham University) for the CONSURF surface modeler.



In Internet I have not find any mathematical description of Alan Ball's 
conic.



I have added some information about the P-value calculation on my web site. 
Please refer to www.ViewsOnCad.ch/VarkonTutorial/conic.htm
