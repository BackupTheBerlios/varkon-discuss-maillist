it would be quite enlighting, to have a list of WP-widgets including
their description. It sure makes a good starting point for porting.

As I wrote to John, one of the difficulties is, that in the process
of module-instantiation, EX can make calls to GUI elements for input
parameters. Not being aware, how this is handled, I suggest to make
an interface, that allows registration of callbacks providing the
wanted information - how the GUI collects this information should
be transparent to EX. I imagine this does not force to big a deviation
of how it is implemented now.
