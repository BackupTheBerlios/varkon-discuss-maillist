Hi Johan,

To use OpenGL with GTK, current practice seems to be GtkGLExt
(http://projects.gnome.org/gtkglext/), replacing the earlier GtkGLArea.
A somewhat illuminating comparison between the two is here:
http://www.mail-archive.com/gtk-app-devel-list@gnome.org/msg10995.html

More generally, I do not have direct experience with the GTK API.
I was skeptical when Armin warned me that moving to GTK would be a big
project, but I was wrong. Usual documentation for GTK and other toolkits
starts with happy assumptions of starting from scratch, missing
the porting challenges.

What I now suggest is a draft plan for moving Varkon to GTK, posting the
plan on the web, and inviting others to find problems with the plan. A
starting point might be a partial point-by-point comparison between WP and
GTK/GtkGLExt.

John
