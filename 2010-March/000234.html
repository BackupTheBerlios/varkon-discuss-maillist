John,

1. Varkon and Autocad are very different systems. I don't
remember exactly what XREF is but it seems close to REF in
VARKON.

2. Applications developed in MBS are compatible between Linux
and Windows. Most of the Varkon sourcecode (C-code) is also
portable. One important exception is the GUI which is based
on X-windows in Linux and WIN32 in Windows. For us, this is
a major issue at the moment. We want to develop a common GUI
for both platforms. Any suggestions ?

3. What is wxwidgets ?
